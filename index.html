Our History

In 2018, Brenden Mattila asked his dad Joel if he could play ice hockey. Joel said "Sure, if I can too!" Neither had ever played hockey before. After one season, Joel and Brenden were hooked, and the Suomi Poikas ("Finnish Boys") were born.

The club is made up primarily of Finnish-American players. Most seasons, there is a Rec League team and a C/CC League team. We play at the Mountain View Ice Arena in Vancouver, Washington, USA.
